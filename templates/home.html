Getting Started
  1. Verify your Splunk connection settings in the Settings page.
  2. Check your field mappings in the Field Mapping page.
  3. Go to the Run Query page to enter a Sigma rule.
  4. Convert the rule to a Splunk query and execute it.
  5. View your past queries and results in the Results page.
